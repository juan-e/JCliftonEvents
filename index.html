About Me

I'm currently studying computer science and business at the University of Michigan. In the past I trained at the Scratch DJ Academy in Los Angeles. I'm a resident DJ at the Study Hall Lounge, a bar and nightclub in Ann Arbor. I'm constantly working to improve my song selection and mixing skills, and you can rest assured that your event's music is in good hands.

Only The Best Equipment

Each piece of equipment in my arsenal was chosen with two things in mind: exceptional sound quality, and durability. At the heart of my setup are two 12-inch powered Alto PA speakers which easily accomodate up to 200 guests. Having a larger event? Not a problem, more sound and lighting equipment is included in the deluxe package.
